We are Vita Innovations, We Create Build Develop Solutions

Born amid the Covid-19 pandemic, our digital health start-up is focused on advancing the future of emergency medicine.

Who We Are

We are a dynamic, multidisciplinary team with an unwavering dedication to advancing the future of emergency medicine. Since our inception, our team has shared a passion for improving patient outcomes, elevating the standard of care, and expanding clinical capacity. Our diverse backgrounds in healthcare, business consulting, engineering, and entrepreneurship provide a unique and holistic perspective. For over three years, we've remained steadfast in our commitment to pioneering the future of e-triage. Our journey reflects a relentless pursuit of innovation in emergency medicine and a nuanced understanding of the ED triage workflows, clinician frustrations, and the intricacies of regulatory processes and EHR interoperability.

Our Motivation

Healthcare is at the forefront of a transformative shift where the integration of AI has become a necessity. With medical data doubling every 73 days, our GenAI-driven technology empowers clinicians to navigate vast datasets, bridge information gaps, and enhance decision-making. This is especially needed in emergency medicine, which experiences record-high staffing shortages and overcrowding.

Our Vision

Our vision is to develop an AI-powered triage companion platform that streamlines patient intake, improves clinical capacity, and provides personalized decision support in crowded emergency department waiting room settings. We hope for our technology to evolve into a cornerstone of emergency medicine, addressing critical inefficiencies ranging from mistriage to inefficient resource allocations, monitoring gaps, and nursing shortages. Rooted in the ED space, we aim to further expand into urgent care, telehealth, and pre-hospital markets, revolutionizing health delivery and outcomes globally.
