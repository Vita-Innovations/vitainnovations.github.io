Our Mission

Our original team (Oscar, Kristen, Ray, and Jason) came together for the 7th Annual Weill Cornell Health Hackathon. After pitching the idea for VitalMask and realizing its potential, we recruited more founders on board. This included Daniel for his programming skills, Sarah for her outreach skills, Rishi for his electrical engineering skills, and Julia for her business skills.

Our Vision

Our original aim was just to see how far we could bring this product to reality. After hearing feedback from doctors who mentioned a need for this idea, we started to see a business potential come up from this idea. In the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic, a smart mask for the Emergency Department seemed more essential than ever.

Why Us

We want to create a smart mask for the emergency department waiting room that can monitor patients' vitals and detect issues even before they arise. Essentially, we hope to play a role in saving lives for patients all around the United States, and eventually all around the world.
