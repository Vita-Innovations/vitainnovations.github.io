About Us

We're a group of Cornell University students and alumni focused on addressing the issues surrounding emergency department overcrowding. With limited personnel and space, patients wait for several hours unmonitored in waiting rooms before receiving care, if at all. Unfortunately, ED overcrowding can often lead to increased walkouts, progression of illness, and fatalities.

VIEW MORE

The Problem

Over 90% of EDs experience overcrowding and 92% of the physicians we reached out to (n=115) are concerned of ED overcrowding and its symptoms.

Our Solution

We are creating a device that will provide continous monitoring for patients in waiting rooms and alert medical personnel of any emergencies.

Hardware

Our device fits under a traditional surgical mask and is equipped with sensors that measure heart rate, spo2 levels, temperature, and respiratory rate.

Software

Our desktop application can connect to multiple devices through bluetooth. The application displays all recorded vitals and allows for custom thresholds alarm thresholds.
